About Us Page

Welcome to College Book Seller, your number one source for all things college textbook related. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of college books, with an emphasis on easy navigation, high product amount, and safe communication.

Founded in 2021 by Bookeepers, College Book Seller has come a long way from its beginnings at Rhode Island College. When Bookeepers first started out, their passion for college textbooks drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely, Bookeepers
